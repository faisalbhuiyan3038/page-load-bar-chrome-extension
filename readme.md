Tired of staring at a blank white screen, wondering if your page is frozen or just loading?

Bring back the visual feedback you've been missing! Load Progress Bar & Page Counter adds a sleek, modern, and fully customizable loading bar to every webpage. But it doesn't stop there—it also includes a Vivaldi-style element counter to show you exactly what's happening under the hood.

Stop guessing and start seeing. You'll get instant confirmation that the page is working, giving you a smarter and less frustrating browsing experience.

## ✨ Why You'll Love It:
- 📈 See Your Progress: A slim, configurable progress bar shows the page's load status.

- 🔢 Vivaldi-Style Counter: Get a live "Loaded / Discovered" count (e.g., 82 / 110) so you know exactly how many scripts, images, and styles are being processed.

- 🎨 Fully Customizable: Make it yours!

- Choose any color you want.

- Enable a beautiful Rainbow Mode.

- Adjust the thickness and opacity.

- Place it at the top or bottom of the page.

- Toggle a smooth animation.

- ✅ Never Guess Again: Instantly know the difference between a slow page and a frozen tab.

- ⚡ Lightweight & Fast: No bloat. Just a simple, focused feature that works.

This extension is inspired by the best features from browsers like Firefox and Vivaldi, bringing them all to Chrome in one simple, must-have package.
